**Your AI-Powered Virtual Agent**

Daiana is SEIDOR Analytics' platform that enables you to create, train and deploy virtual agents powered by artificial intelligence.

From conversational assistants to complex customer service systems or process automation, Daiana offers a comprehensive, scalable and adaptable solution for every need.

<div class="box_img">
![Daiana](/img/daiana/index.jpeg)
</div>

Thanks to its low-code approach and powerful flow orchestrator, you can build custom agents in minutes. Additionally, it integrates multiple knowledge sources, such as documents, databases or cloud tools, enabling precise and contextual responses.

**Daiana doesn't just respond: it understands, learns and acts.**

## How does DAIANA work?
### Creation of knowledge areas
Chatbots **specialized by areas**. Human resources, sales, support, customer service, finance.

### Document loading and management
With constant access to proprietary data **(Docs, PDF, TXT, databases)** these chatbots offer intelligent and specialized assistance.

### Learning and training
Using LLM APIs like GPT and GEMINI to allow chatbots to continuously learn and improve their response capabilities and understanding.

### Query
Interact with virtual assistants naturally, asking questions and receiving precise and contextually relevant answers.

## Intuitive Interface
<div class="box_ui">
![Daiana](/img/daiana/ui.png)
</div>

An easy-to-use interface that simplifies the agent creation process. Easy to use, simple to scale.

- **Centralized Agent Management**: Organize all your agents by teams, projects or areas within collaborative workspaces. Manage and access each one from a single place.
- **Knowledge Bases**: Centralize and organize all your information sources in one environment. Create bases with multiple sources (documents, databases or cloud connections) or reuse sources between different agents according to your needs.
- **Comprehensive Agent Administration**: Control the behavior, configurations and publications of all your virtual assistants from a single panel, without losing visibility or control.
- **History, Reports and Real-Time Data**: Access complete conversations of each agent, analyze interactions, obtain key metrics and make continuous improvements with instantly updated information.
