**Cloud Cost Management**

Help companies manage and optimize their cloud service expenses.

Provides a clear and detailed view of costs associated with cloud resource usage, enabling organizations to make informed and strategic decisions.

In addition to offering financial control, Cloud Costing acts as a precise meter for cloud consumption, identifying in real-time which services are being used, by whom, and with what intensity.

This visibility allows not only cost optimization but also promotes a culture of responsible and efficient use of technological resources.

## Benefits
- **Cost Optimization:** Identifies savings areas and optimizes cloud resource usage.
- **Financial Transparency:** Provides detailed and customized reports on cloud spending.
- **Scalability:** Adapts to your company's changing needs, allowing hassle-free growth
- **Security and Compliance:** Ensures data and processes comply with security regulations and standards.

## Use Cases

### Cost Distribution by Area
Allows assigning and visualizing cloud expenses by areas, business units, or projects, facilitating profitability analysis and correct cost allocation.

### Cloud Cost Consolidation
Unifies expenses from multiple cloud providers (such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud) in a single centralized view, simplifying multicloud financial management.

### Cloud Service Consumption Analysis
Provides a detailed breakdown of specific service usage by provider, allowing identification of optimization opportunities and reduction of unnecessary consumption.

### Budget Cost Measurement and Control
Compares incurred costs with budgeted costs in real-time, alerting to deviations and helping maintain financial control of the cloud environment.
