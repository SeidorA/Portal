**Feelings First**

**Feelings** is an active listening solution for major social networks that helps determine your customers' sentiments.

From an account, post, or comments related to a topic or theme, it detects positive, negative, and neutral sentiments and allows you to analyze them graphically.

**Feelings builds active listening** as it allows you to analyze the most relevant cases in detail and respond to each one of them.


## Active Listening
**Listen to what your customers have to say**

In today's social media world, consumers freely express their emotions about brands, products, and solutions.

This is an excellent opportunity to hear firsthand what your customers have to say and help your company and products improve day by day.

**Active listening on social networks** is the differentiator that your brand can have over competitors.


## Features
You can select any topic you want and quickly see public opinion on social networks
- **KPIs and Evolution** You can see the most relevant emotion indicators and how they evolved over time (even minute by minute)
- **Word Analysis** You can quickly see the most relevant words in both positive and negative phrases
- **Comment Details** You can access each individual post and sort them by different attributes
- **Response Automation** In the Enterprise version, you can automate responses based on the nature of comments
- **Compare with your competitors** Analyze your networks and comments versus those of your competitors and gain a competitive advantage
