DOXA by SEIDOR Analytics is a solution that enables businesses and individuals to create, distribute and analyze surveys and questionnaires in a simple, fast and efficient way.

With a user-friendly interface and advanced analytics tools, DOXA positions itself as the ideal solution for collecting feedback and obtaining valuable insights.

## How does DOXA work?

### User-Friendly Survey Builder
Allows users to **create custom surveys** by dragging and dropping different types of questions (multiple choice, short answer, Likert scale, etc.).

### Pre-designed Templates
Offers a variety of **templates for different purposes** (customer satisfaction, product feedback, internal surveys, etc.) that users can customize according to their needs.

### Multichannel Distribution
Users can **share their surveys** via email, social media, QR codes or embed them on their websites.

### Real-Time Analysis
As responses are collected, users can view graphs and statistics in **real-time**, facilitating decision-making.

### Response Segmentation
Allows filtering and **segmenting** responses according to different criteria, such as demographics, specific answers, among others.

### Integrations
**Connectivity** with popular CRM, marketing and analytics tools for better data management.

## Other Features

### Accessibility and Responsive Design
Surveys display perfectly on any device, ensuring respondents can answer comfortably from mobile phones, tablets or computers.

### Security
End-to-end data **encryption** and compliance with international privacy regulations.

### Team Collaboration
Users can work as a **team** on the same survey, assigning specific roles and permissions.

### Data Export
Users can **export** results in different formats (CSV, PDF, Excel) for more detailed analysis.

## Intuitive Interface
An easy-to-use interface that simplifies the survey creation process.

Easy to use, simple to scale

- **Results Dashboard:** Visualize responses in real-time with filters by segment, trend or keyword.
- **Sentiment and Topic Analysis:** Extract qualitative insights with natural language processing algorithms.
- **Advanced Segmentation:** Cross-reference data to understand behaviors by profile, area or location.
- **Report Automation:** Receive customized reports directly in your email or BI platform.
